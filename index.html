About Us.

Welcome to PT. Biru Sejahtera Nusantara, a newly established company in the field of exports. We are thrilled to introduce ourselves as a fresh player dedicated to delivering top-quality export services in this industry. With a steadfast commitment to sustainability and innovation, we are poised to bring the natural treasures of the archipelago to the global stage, offering the finest quality and satisfying customer experiences. Together, let's synergize for success and unlock new opportunities in international trade.

Vision.

To become a leader in the export industry of palm kernel and its derivatives, strengthening Indonesia's position as one of the leading producers in the global market. With a focus on sustainability and innovation, we are committed to providing high-quality products that bring positive benefits to the environment and society.

Mission.

1. Empowering farmers and partners.

2. Providing high-quality products and excellent service.

3. Practicing sustainable and eco-friendly export.

4. Contributing to community development.
